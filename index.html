About SYMBIOT

SYMBIOT is VVCE's flagship 24-hour deep tech hackathon where engineering discipline, system-level thinking, and real-world applicability come together.

Build Beyond Prototypes

SYMBIOT pushes teams to go beyond pitch decks and demo-only hacks. The focus is on deployable hardware and software stacks that can be tested, benchmarked, and scaled.

From embedded systems and robotics to AI-driven platforms, every solution is evaluated for engineering rigor and real-world impact.

Frequently Asked Questions

Key information to help you plan your SYMBIOT 2026 experience.

Who can participate?

SYMBIOT is open to undergraduate and postgraduate students, innovators and early-career engineers from across India with a passion for deep technology.

What domains are covered?

Expect deep-tech problem statements across AI/ML, IoT, robotics, cyber-physical systems, edge computing and more.

Is hardware support provided?

Selected teams will have access to on-campus labs, basic prototyping components and mentoring support. You are encouraged to bring any specialized hardware.

How do I register?

Use the registration link shared by VVCE or reach out via email. For queries, contact the organizing team using the details below.
